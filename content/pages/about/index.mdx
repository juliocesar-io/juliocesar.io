I’m a software engineer from Colombia, Head of AI at [Third Way Health](https://thirdway.health/) and founder/open source maintainer for fun at [Fastfold AI](https://fastfold.ai/). M.Sc. in biomedical engineering at [Uniandes](https://uniandes.edu.co/en), researching AI drug design with small peptides.

Before that, I worked at [Globant](https://www.globant.com/) as a Python/Bioscientist, where I was part of the founding team of the [Life Sciences Studio](https://www.globant.com/studio/healthcare-life-sciences). My work included developing medical imaging with [Nvidia Clara](https://www.nvidia.com/en-us/clara/) for hospitals, and collaborating with [Verily](https://verily.com/) (an Alphabet company) on a [FHIR](https://fhir.org/) implementation for Clinical Pathways.
